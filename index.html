COMPUTERS APPRECIATION PAGE

Computers are one of the most powerful devices humans have ever created. They're able accomplish things us humans could only dream of. They also play a huge impact on our lives; it would be hard to imagine a world without them! Since they were created in the 1800's, they've grown exponentially to the point where they fit in our very own pockets. But they weren't always like this.

  • 1936 - Turing Machine: Many consider as the very first computer
  • 1941 - Z3 Computer: First programmable digital computer
  • 1944 - Colussus: First computer that used binary code
  • 1951 - UNIVAC I: First commercial computer
  • 1956 - IBM 305 RAMAC: Introduced us to the hard disk drive
  • 1971 - Intel 4004: The first microprocessor
  • 1977 - Apple II: The beginning of a brand the took the world by storm
  • 1984 - Apple Macintosh: First graphical interface
  • 1936 - World Wide Web: Information can now be shared within a few seconds
  • 2007 - iPhone: Now fits in your pocket!
